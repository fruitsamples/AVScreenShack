### AVScreenShack ###

===========================================================================
DESCRIPTION:

AVScreenShack demonstrates how to use AVCaptureScreenInput to capture a QuickTime movie.

AVScreenShack exercises the APIs of AVCaptureScreenInput and demonstrates
how they interact with an AVCaptureVideoPreviewLayer and AVCaptureMovieFileOutput.
You can capture the whole screen, or a portion of it, to a QuickTime movie.

===========================================================================
BUILD REQUIREMENTS:

Xcode 4.1 or later, Mac OS X v10.7 or later
